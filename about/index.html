About me

Zhang Peng is a seasoned cybersecurity professional holding CISSP and PMP certifications. He earned his Master’s degree from Liaoning Technical University in 2010. Zhang has successfully managed several key cybersecurity component projects, including those involving:
- In-Vehicle Infotainment (IVI)
- Data Storage System for Automotive Data (DSSAD)
- Telematics Box (TBOX) with built-in Vehicle-to-Everything (V2X)
- Advanced Driver-Assistance Systems (ADAS)
With a strong track record in leading complex cybersecurity initiatives, Zhang ensures robust security and reliability across various vehicle components.
